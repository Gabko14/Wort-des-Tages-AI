# Privacy Policy for Wort des Tages

**Last updated:** December 19, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how "Wort des Tages" ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Device Identifier

We collect an anonymous **device identifier** to:

- Track your premium subscription status
- Restore purchases if you reinstall the app or switch devices

This identifier does not contain personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

### Purchase Information

When you subscribe to premium features, we store:

- Subscription status (active/expired)
- Subscription expiration date
- Purchase tokens (for validation with Google Play)

This information is used solely to provide you with premium features.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Verify and manage your premium subscription
- Restore purchases across devices
- Improve app functionality

We do **not** use your data for:

- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- User profiling or tracking

## Third-Party Services

### Google Play Billing

Subscription purchases are processed through Google Play. Google's privacy policy applies to payment processing: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Supabase (Data Storage)

Your subscription data is securely stored using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

### Sentry (Crash Reporting)

We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. No personal data is included in crash reports. [Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

## Data Retention

We retain your device identifier and subscription data for as long as you have an active subscription. After your subscription expires, data may be retained for up to 12 months for purchase restoration purposes.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Database access is restricted and authenticated
- We do not store sensitive payment information (handled by Google Play)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request information about the data we store
- Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
